The one rule

It never invents facts. No fabricated numbers, metrics, employers, credentials, or events — every factual claim in the output comes from your input. The inflation is 100% framing: paradoxes, epiphanies, lessons, and questions carry zero information, which is exactly the joke.

That's also the design test: feed the output back to deflate and you should get your original fact back — and nothing else. Cringe is the goal; fraud is out of scope.

And if you ask it why a trick works, it drops the bit and answers from the same grounded research catalog as its good twin: references/catalog.md — Cialdini, Pennycook, Green & Brock, Tversky & Kahneman, sources cited.
